My Approach

Functionality

Functionality is one of the most important aspect of coding. If it looks pretty but doesn't work, then I'm not finished yet.

Collaboration

Working with a diverse group of people is a great reicpe for success. Everyone has something unique to bring to the table and I enjoy the process of developing those ideas into fruition.

User Experience

Web development should always be focued on the user's experience. I ask myself if the feartures I've added are intuitive, and easy to navigate.
